Styles and Their Unique Rarities

In Blue Lock Rivals, each player possesses a unique style with varying degrees of rarity, influencing their in-game abilities and potential.

  • World Class Rarest
  • Mythic Very Rare
  • Legendary Rare
  • Epic Uncommon
  • Rare Common

World Class Styles Rarest

  • Sae - Unrivaled technical mastery and vision.
  • Kaiser - Incredible scoring prowess and 'Kaiser Impact' shot.
  • Don Lorenzo - A defensive titan with exceptional interception skills.
  • NEL Isagi - Evolved meta-vision and spatial awareness.

Mythic Styles Very Rare

Epic Styles Uncommon
