Character Styles Information

Dive into the world of character styles in Blue Lock Rivals and understand their rarities, how to obtain them, and some of the most sought-after styles!

Style Rarities:

  • World Class: Extremely rare (0.25% chance)
  • Mythic: Very rare
  • Legendary: Rare
  • Epic: Uncommon
  • Rare: Common

Obtainment:

  • Style Spins: Each spin costs 2500 Yen.
  • Legendary Pity: After 50 normal spins, your next spin is guaranteed to be Legendary or higher.
  • Normal Spin Odds: Rare (62.25%), Epic (30%), Legendary (7%), Mythic (0.5%), World Class (0.25%).
  • Lucky Spins: Higher chances for top rarities - Legendary (92.5%), Mythic (5%), World Class (2.5%).
